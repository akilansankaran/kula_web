Could you spend more time with your loved ones during the work day?

Many of us lose touch because the friction to reconnect is simply too high. Every tool is active, requiring you to initiate, coordinate, and remember; but when you're running at full capacity, such tools become barriers.

What is Kula?

A signal. Omnipresence. Prioritising your loved ones.

Three simple states. No words required. Your circle, your sense of time.

Available

A ring with a central dot. You're free; present; and open to connection. We'll find you the time to connect.

Unavailable

An empty ring. You're in focus mode. No interruption, no guilt.

Pulse

A filled, pulsing circle. Sent intentionally. A single tap that signals: I'm here, and I'm thinking of you.

Kula knows when you're available.

Connected to your calendar, meetings, and tasks: we preserve your privacy.

Our mission

We built Kula because we lived it.

Between the relentless pace of lectures and deadlines, the people we love most - parents across time zones; siblings; and friends with their own ambitions - started to feel further away. Because finding the same open window felt impossible.

The time is there. It's always been there. The problem is that neither side knows when the other has it.

Kula closes that gap. Not with relentless notifications, but with light. Ambient. Passive. Always present. A signal that makes your availability visible to the people who matter most, so the moments you do share are no longer left to chance.
